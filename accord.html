What is CBD?

Cannabidiol (CBD) is a non-intoxicating Cannabinoid found in the flowers of the hemp plant. CBD was once over-shadowed by THC (the compound in cannabis sativa that causes a buzz)

Is CBD Legal in the UK?

Yes. CBD is legal in the UK if sold as a food supplement. CBD products that are produced using Cannabis Sativa L. (hemp) are able to be legally sold as food supplements.

What is CBD used for?

CBD can help with mental issues such as Anxiety and depression as well as sleeping disorders. It can also be used to alleviate pain.

Which CBD Product to use?

The most effective way to take CBD is via a spray because it is absorbed into the bloodstream far quicker via the sublingual ducts, making it faster acting, and more efficient.
